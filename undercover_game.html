Undercover

Find the spy. Survive the vote.

How to play

🟢 Civilians — share the same secret word
🟣 Undercover — have a similar but different word
🟡 Mr. White — has no word at all
Each round, everyone gives a one-word clue about their word, then votes to eliminate the most suspicious player. Civilians win when all Undercover & Mr. White are gone. Spies win if they survive to the final 3, or if Mr. White correctly guesses the civilian word when eliminated.

Players

Add 5–8 players and assign roles.
